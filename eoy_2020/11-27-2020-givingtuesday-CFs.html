Thanksgiving may have been yesterday but we want to carry the spirit for a few more days. This coming Tuesday is #GivingTuesday, a day created in response to the consumption of Black Friday and Cyber Monday to focus on giving instead of spending.

#GivingTuesday has become the traditional start of the end of year fundraising season for Casey Trees and many other nonprofits. But this year we want to start this season of giving by expressing our gratitude. Like everyone we’ve had to adjust how we do things this year with the COVID-19 pandemic. The largest change that we’ve had to make has been shifting from volunteer-based tree planting events to relying exclusively on staff and crew to plant our trees. While we’ve always been grateful for our Citizen Foresters and other volunteers, this year without you has really brought into focus how important you are to our mission.

Over the next month, we’ll be highlighting some of the great work that our tree planting crew has been able to accomplish over the past year. But on #GivingTuesday it’s all about our volunteers, how thankful we are for all you’ve done for us over the years, and how much we’re looking forward to planting with you again in the future!

Thank you for all that you do to support our mission!

Regards,
