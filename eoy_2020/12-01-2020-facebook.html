CASEY TREES / # GIVING TUESDAY

12/1/2020

Today, #GivingTuesday, your gift goes further towards keeping DC green and vibrant.

Facebook is matching the first $2 million in donations made on their platform and then 10% of donations until they’ve given an additional $5 million. Donate early and often to help us not only take advantage of this generous offer, but also to help us continue planting trees without the help of our beloved volunteers.

We’ve learned a lot this year. We’ve all adjusted how we work and I’m truly inspired by how our crew continued to work towards our mission while keeping our community and each other safe. And we’ve learned how much we miss our loyal volunteers when we can’t plant with them and how much we can’t wait to plant with them again.

I’m asking you to make a gift today in honor of your favorite volunteer to support the tree planting crews still working to re-tree DC, and the urban trees whose lives they make possible.

All you need to do is head to our Facebook Fundraiser and give.

You could give:

  • Give $11, one dollar representing each of our crew members
  • Give $22, one dollar for each park we’ve planted in this year
  • Give $40, representing the city’s goal of 40 percent canopy goal we’re hustling towards
  • Give $46, one dollar for each Community or School Tree Planting andPark Inventory event we had to cancel
  • Give $196, one dollar for each Citizen Forester we haven’t gotten to plant with this year
  • Give $558, one dollar each student or youth we virtually interacted with (the future!)

Donate whatever you can, but please be as generous as possible. Even though we’ve had to do it a little differently lately, the work we do is still vital to trees. Don’t have a Facebook? Don’t want to donate there? Head to caseytrees.org/donate all day to contribute. We’re so grateful for your support. Your donation today allows us to continue this work – because trees need us, and we need you.
