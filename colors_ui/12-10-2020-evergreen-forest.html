CASEY TREES / EVERGREEN

Our evergreen members make all the difference.

Something that’s been very clear to me throughout this tumultuous year is the constant presence of trees. As I take my daily walks around my tree-lined neighborhood, their presence has been a comforting constant in my life. The positive psychological effects of trees and green spaces has never been as evident as it has in the past year.

But, as vital as trees are to our urban lives, cities aren’t the easiest places for them to live. While humans are the cause of many of the stresses on urban trees, we’re also the ones who can help.

Trees don’t just face these threats at the end of the year when we ask for your support - the threats they face are just as year-round and constant as the trees themselves. That’s why I’m asking you to commit to a monthly recurring gift today. The work we do to make DC a more liveable place for trees lasts throughout the year and your support should too.

If you were planning on making a year-end gift, why not divide that number by twelve and commit to a monthly gift of that amount? Our evergreen members give us, and trees, that constant reliable source of support throughout the year.

Thank you for supporting trees throughout the year.

Regards,

Mark Buscaino
